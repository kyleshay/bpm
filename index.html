Quick Tap for BPM tool!

Works on any site.

How to use:

  1. Drag this link: bpm to your bookmark bar.
  2. Click the bpm bookmarklet with any webpage open from your bookmarks bar.
  3. Tap any key to the beat of a song to determine the BPM.
